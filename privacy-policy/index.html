    Privacy Policy

    Last Updated: September 9, 2024

    Introduction¶

    Welcome to Kelas Utsman. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and protection of data.

    1. Information We Do Not Collect¶

    At Kelas Utsman, we prioritize your privacy by not collecting any personal information. This includes, but is not limited to:

    • Personal identification information (such as name, date of birth, or government-issued ID numbers)
    • Contact information (including email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses)
    • Financial information (credit card details, bank account information, or any payment-related data)
    • Usage data (IP addresses, browser type, or device information)
    • Cookies or any other tracking technologies
    • Location data
    • Biometric data
    • Social media information

    2. Why We Don't Collect Information¶

    We believe in providing our services while respecting your right to privacy. By not collecting any personal data, we ensure that your information remains completely private and secure.

    3. Use of Information¶

    As we do not collect any personal information, there is no use, processing, or sharing of user data by Kelas Utsman. We do not:

    • Use your data for marketing purposes
    • Share your information with third parties
    • Analyze user behavior or preferences
    • Create user profiles

    4. Data Storage and Security¶

    Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there are no data storage or security measures in place for user information. This means:

    • No risk of data breaches affecting your personal information
    • No need for data encryption or protection mechanisms
    • No data retention policies, as we don't retain any data

    5. Third-Party Services and Links¶

    Kelas Utsman does not use any third-party services that may collect information about you. However, our website or services may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

    6. Children's Privacy¶

    Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13, also known as "Children" under COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers (if we had any, which we don't).

    7. Changes to This Privacy Policy¶

    We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

    8. Your Rights Under Data Protection Laws¶

    As we do not collect any personal data, many typical data protection rights do not apply when using our services. However, you always have the right to:

    • Be informed about how your data is collected and used (which in this case, it isn't)
    • Request information about any personal data we might hold about you (which we don't)
    • Request deletion of any data (which isn't necessary as we don't hold any)

    9. International Data Transfers¶

    As we do not collect or store any personal information, there are no international data transfers conducted by Kelas Utsman.

    10. Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data Under GDPR¶

    As we do not collect or process any personal data, we do not rely on any legal basis for data processing under the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

    11. California Privacy Rights¶

    Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. However, as Kelas Utsman does not collect any personal information, these rights are not applicable in the context of our services.

    12. Contact Us¶

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

    Kelas Utsman Email: utsmannn@gmail.com

    13. Consent¶

    By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. You understand that we do not collect any personal information and therefore cannot use, share, or process any such data.
